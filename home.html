About Us

Specializing in all Hair textures including Straight, Wavy, Curly, and Kinky / Coily, Helio Salon is all about making the best of what is naturally yours or adding/subtracting textural elements (as with Healthy Perms or Relaxing treatments). With over 15 years of dedicated experience we are all about helping our clients achieve their hair goals while at the same time educating on the best products, and hair care + styling techniques to maintain your style.

Created by Celebrity Stylist and Former Vidal Sassoon Salon Educator Kaeleigh Benson, Helio Hair Salon reinvents the way people think about and experience their own hair in a private, low-key, friendly environment. Natural, sustainable products align with fashion to create a new approach to beauty.

As healthy hair is an essential part of our mission, every product we use is plant based, organic, fair-trade, and most are vegan. Everything is sourced direct to ensure its active natural ingredients are at their peek- something like Farm to Chair. Additionally, we offer an ever-growing range of Hair-Wellness programs including our Non-toxic Keratin, Damaged Hair Rebuilding treatments, and Organic Color.
